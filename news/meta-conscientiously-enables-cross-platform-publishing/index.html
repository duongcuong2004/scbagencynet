Meta enables cross-platform publishing

Facebook, Google, Insights 9/6/2024 pm2:53
  1. SCB AgencyHome
  2. Insights
  3. Facebook

1.Main features and benefits of the feature: Improve efficiency: Reduce duplication of work, users do not need to create and publish content on each platform separately.
Expand influence: Through simultaneous publishing on multiple platforms, it can reach a wider audience.
Maintain consistency: Ensure the consistency of content style and information delivery on each platform to enhance brand image.
Data analysis integration: It is convenient to track and analyze content performance across platforms to provide a basis for strategic adjustments.


2.Setting steps and interface display:
How to cross-post Instagram pictures to Threads:
Users turn on the Threads share button when posting pictures. Once users cross-post Instagram posts, the title will become the text of the Threads post, and the hashtags will be converted to plain text.
Users can choose to use the cross-post option once, or set automatic sharing to always cross-post to Threads. This feature is available to all iOS and Android device users worldwide.
However, there is no option to cross-post Instagram Reels to Threads.

How to cross-post Threads posts to Facebook:
Similar to how users turn on the Instagram cross-posting option, there is a new Threads option that users can enable when typing a post.
At the top, next to options like Only Me and Add Album, users should see the Threads logo with a status of On or Off.
The feature only works for text and link posts, not videos. The feature is available on Android, iOS, and in all countries where Threads is supported.

3. Functional limitations and precautions
Instructions for not being able to publish Reels across platforms at present:
Due to the specific format and algorithm optimization of Reels (short video function on Instagram), cross-platform publishing may not be fully supported at present. Users need to create and publish Reels content on each platform separately.
Compatibility issues between Facebook videos and Threads:
Facebook videos may encounter compatibility issues when published across platforms due to format, resolution or copyright restrictions.
Threads (Facebooks private messaging app) may not currently be supported as a target platform for cross-platform publishing, and users need to be aware of this.

Conversion rules for post titles and hashtags when posting from Instagram across platforms:
Titles may be automatically adjusted or truncated based on the character limits of each platform.
Hashtags are usually retained when posting across platforms, but be aware of some platforms restrictions on the number of hashtags or specific rules (such as Twitters #FirstWordMustBeHashtag).
Some special characters or links may be parsed differently on different platforms, so it is recommended to test in advance to ensure the correct display of content.

The impact of Meta cross-platform publishing on advertisers

Metas official launch of the cross-platform publishing function for Facebook, Instagram and Threads has undoubtedly brought far-reaching impacts to advertisers.
The following are several major aspects of the impact on advertisers:
1 Improve content dissemination efficiency and coverage

  1. Content synchronization and time saving: Advertisers can now more conveniently publish advertising content or brand information to multiple social platforms under Meta, without having to edit and publish content separately for each platform, thereby significantly improving the efficiency of content dissemination.
    According to Metas official data, this feature helped users save an average of 30% of publishing time during the testing phase.
  2. Expand coverage: Through cross-platform publishing, advertisers can ensure that content reaches user groups on Facebook, Instagram and Threads at the same time, thereby expanding brand exposure and influence.
    This wide coverage helps to increase brand awareness and attract more potential customers.
    2 Optimize advertising strategies and effect evaluation
  3. Customized content adjustment: Although cross-platform publishing provides convenience, advertisers also need to make appropriate customized adjustments to content based on the characteristics of each platform and differences in user behavior.

At the same time, by optimizing advertising strategies and improving content dissemination efficiency, advertisers can use advertising budgets more effectively and reduce overall marketing costs.
For example, Instagram focuses on visual content, while Threads focuses more on short, real-time text updates.
Through the cross-platform publishing function, advertisers can more easily test the performance of different content on different platforms, thereby optimizing advertising strategies.

  1. Effect evaluation and data analysis: Meta provides a wealth of data analysis tools to help advertisers monitor the performance of cross-platform content.
    Advertisers can evaluate user interaction rates, conversion rates and other indicators on different platforms based on data feedback, and then adjust advertising strategies to improve advertising effects.
    3 Reduce costs and improve ROI
  2. Cost savings: The cross-platform publishing function reduces the time and labor costs required for advertisers to publish content on multiple platforms.
    At the same time, by optimizing advertising strategies and improving content dissemination efficiency, advertisers can use advertising budgets more effectively and reduce overall marketing costs.
  3. Improve ROI: With the improvement of content dissemination efficiency and coverage, advertisers are expected to obtain a higher return on investment (ROI).
    Through precise advertising and effective content dissemination, advertisers can attract more potential customers and promote purchasing behavior, thereby increasing business revenue.
    4 Challenges and precautions
  4. Content adaptability issues: User behavior and content preferences on different platforms vary, and cross-platform publishing may lead to content adaptability issues.
    For example, the display effect of topic tags in Threads is not good. Advertisers need to pay close attention to the content performance on each platform and make adjustments as needed.
  5. Advertising policy and compliance: Advertisers must also comply with Metas advertising guidelines and community guidelines when publishing across platforms.
    Illegal content may result in consequences such as ad deletion or account ban.
    Therefore, advertisers need to ensure the compliance of advertising content and pay close attention to Metas policy dynamics.
