A complete guide to troubleshooting Facebook accounts

  1. SCB AgencyHome
  2. Insights
  3. Facebook

Recently, there are many users who have consulted about Facebook account issues. It can be seen that when placing Facebook ads in China, accounts are not working and are often blocked. This article summarizes some of the problems that have been reported by many people recently. If you have similar troubles, you can try to solve them with the accounts provided by our system.

1. Why is it that the account is suspended when running general goods? Facebook advertising risk control is not only for the Black Five products. There are also rules and restrictions on the promotion of general goods. So don’t think that general goods can be unscrupulous. There is still a risk of being controlled and blocked. Before placing, you must also carefully understand the rules and regulations of the platform. When the risk control is strict, there will be situations where it is blocked for no reason. Under normal circumstances, you can appeal back, but it may take a long time and energy. If you want to place general goods stably, Yuli Gai can meet it. The system has built-in domestic two unlimited corporate accounts. If the account is blocked, we will help you file an appeal and automatically add an account. Yuli Gai supports cross-account copying of ads. During the appeal period, you can copy high-quality ads to the new account for promotion to avoid the suspension of ads caused by the account being blocked, which affects the sales of goods.

2. Why is the account suspended as soon as the card is bound? One is that the account quality is not good or the environment is unstable. Another is that the card head is a problem. A card head can only be used for a period of time. If it is used for a long time, it will be marked by FB, resulting in the account being suspended after the card is bound. When using the Yurigai system for delivery, there is no need to prepare your own FB account, agent, virtual card, etc. We will help you prepare a stable environment and account. You only need to recharge and deliver, without spending too much energy and money on the initial preparation.

3. Why is the advertising account suspended? It is very likely that the material has been marked. After the account is blocked, we must first check whether it is due to the material. If it is due to the material, it is easy to cause the new account to be blocked without changing the material and changing the account. Another reason is that the account is too new and unstable. It is easy to be blocked if it is used directly for advertising. You can try to use a billing account. The so-called billing account refers to an account that has been used to deliver advertisements and has generated bills. It is relatively stable. If you need a billing account, you can add a customer service at the end of the article for details.

4. What should I do if the BM of the connected account is suspended? If BM is blocked, pixels will not be usable, and all advertising accounts under the BM will be blocked, which is quite serious. Recently, BM risk control is quite strict. It is best not to touch the previously stable BM, and do not add new accounts to it to avoid affecting the accounts being placed. Using the Yuri Gai automated advertising delivery system, which comes with a cloak, can greatly reduce the risk of advertising. The Black Five products that are prohibited by the platform can also be used with confidence. According to member feedback, the probability of account blocking is reduced by more than 80%, so the security of BM has also been improved.

5. How can I appeal back if my account is blocked? First, you need to confirm the reason for the account being blocked. If it is due to a banned product, the probability of appealing back is very small. For other reasons, you need to explain in the appeal that you have rectified it and promised not to commit it again. The probability of a successful appeal is still relatively high. If you dont know how to appeal, you can directly use our Yuri Gai. If your account is blocked, we will help you appeal. The success rate and efficiency are very high. If the appeal fails, you will also be provided with a new account, eliminating the trouble of your account being blocked.
